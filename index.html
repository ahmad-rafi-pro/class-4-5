GIGABYTE GeForce RTX­­ 4070 Ti GAMING OC 12GB GDDR6X Graphics Card

The GIGABYTE GeForce RTX 4070 Ti GAMING OC is a powerful graphics card for gaming and demanding multimedia applications. It comes with a core clock speed of 2640 MHz and it features 7680 CUDA cores and 12 GB of GDDR6X memory with a clock speed of 21 Gbps and a 192-bit memory bus. The card is PCI-E 4.0 compliant and can support digital resolutions of up to 7680x4320. It also has 3 DisplayPort 1.4 outputs and 1 HDMI 2.1 output. It has 4 multi-view capabilities and measures 336mm in length, 140mm in width, and 58mm in height. It has an ATX PCB form factor and is compatible with DirectX 12 Ultimate and OpenGL 4.6. A recommended power supply of 750W is required, and the card has a single 16-pin power connector.

Windforce Cooling System

The GIGABYTE GeForce RTX­­ 4070 Ti GAMING OC 12G comes with WINDFORCE cooling system that features three 100mm unique blade fans, alternate spinning, 7 composite copper heat pipes, a vapor chamber directly touches the GPU, 3D active fans, Screen cooling, which together provide high-efficiency heat dissipation. Alternate spinning reduces the turbulence of adjacent fans and increases airflow pressure. The 3D Active Fan provides semi-passive cooling, and the fans will remain off when the GPU is in a low-load or low-power game. The airflow is spilled by the triangular fan edge and guided smoothly through the 3D stripe curve on the fan surface.
